---
title: Joint Statement on U.S. Customs and Border Protection Presence at UNC-Chapel Hill
---

Today the UNC-Chapel Hill chapter of the AAUP, UE 150, and TransparUNCy sent [a joint statement](/assets/joint-statement-on-uscbp-at-unc-ch.pdf) to Chancellor Roberts, Interim Provost Dean, Dean White, and Dean Rieckenberg demanding that UNC Chapel Hill ensure the safety of its students and staff in the face of domestic terrorism.
